My name is Dewald and I was born and raised in Cape Town South-Africa. I finished High School in 1999 and have been employed in various fields over the years. I spent some time in London where I experienced different cultures and also did a variety of interesting jobs. After I came back home to South-Africa I enrolled for a Cullinary Cooking course which I completed after also working full-time in restaurants and for catering companies. I then moved into sales and more specifically retail. Currently I am employed as a full time Sales Consultant in a high-end Jewellery store.
I enjoy working with other people and providing excellent service to clients is a big passion for me. I decided to enroll for an online Web Development course through HyperionDev as a way to expose myself to the exciting field of web development. I enjoy the endless possibilities in creating something unique and beautiful. Creating a balance between simplicity, aesthetics and functionality is what inspires me.
